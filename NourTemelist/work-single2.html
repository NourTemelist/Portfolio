Matchy Matchy

Matchy Mathcy is an innovative mobile application that enables users to communicate with a chatbot. The app is designed to understand users' preferences through both descriptive text and image inputs, offering a seamless and interactive experience. It provides a user-friendly chat interface where users can engage in conversations with a chatbot. The chatbot is trained to understand and respond to user requests and preferences related to clothes.

Users can convey their preferences and requirements by typing descriptive text messages within the chat interface. The chatbot is made with Rasa framework and Spacy library utilizing natural language processing techniques to understand the text input accurately. Users can specify their preferences in detail, such as clothes features. In addition to descriptive text, Chatbot allows users to transmit their preferences via images. Users can capture photos or select images from their device's gallery to communicate their preferences visually. For example, if a user wants to find a specific clothing item, they can simply take a photo and share it with the chatbot.


The app leverages advanced image similarity technology to analyze and fetch for the similar images to the ones shared by users. The chatbot uses computer vision algorithms and models such Yolov7 and U-net to extract relevant information from the images, enabling it to detect each clothing item within the picture. This functionality enhances the app's ability to provide accurate and personalized output.


Based on the user's preferences expressed through text or images, the chatbot generates personalized recommendations. It utilizes recommendation algorithm to analyze user data and respond to its preferences.
