Appliances Shop

The homepage of Appliances Shop features a clean and user-friendly design, with many displayed categories and search bar at the top for easy product discovery.Once you click on product, Each one includes product images, a detailed description, price, available colors, customer ratings and the option to add the product to the shopping cart or the favourite cart.

The shopping cart page provides a summary of the selected items, their prices, and the total cost where Users can adjust quantitites by removing or adding products.


Clicking on the "Sign Up" button takes the user to a registration page. Once registered and logged in, users have access to their account dashboard, where they can manage their profile, shipping addresses, payment methods, and order history.
